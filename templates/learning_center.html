Technique Library

The Smash

Master the downward power shot. Focus on shoulder rotation and high contact point.

Pro Tip: Snap your wrist at the peak!

The Clear

The defensive lob. Aim for the back baseline with full arm extension.

Pro Tip: Finish your swing across the body.

The Drop

Deceptive slow shot. Use the same prep as a smash but soften the contact.

Pro Tip: Brush the shuttlecock for steepness.

The Net Shot

Front court finesse. Control the height and tumble over the net cord.

Pro Tip: Keep your arm relaxed and elbow high.

📐 Biomechanical Checkpoints

Phase 1: Preparation
  • Non-racket arm pointing at shuttle
  • Shoulders perpendicular to net
  • Weight on back foot
  • Racket elbow at 90°
Phase 2: Swing/Contact
  • Full elbow extension at contact
  • Core rotation initiated
  • Wrist snap (Pronation)
  • High contact point (11 o'clock)
